Vegan Burger

Description

Vegan burger is a plant-based alternative to the traditional beef burger, made with ingredients like soy protein, vegetables, and herbs.

Ingredients

  • Soy protein
  • Vegetables
  • Herbs
  • Butter
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Mustard
  • Ketchup

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Roll out dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread cinnamon and sugar mixture evenly on dough.
  4. Roll up dough tightly and secure with toothpicks.
  5. Place in a baking dish and brush with melted butter.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until golden brown.
