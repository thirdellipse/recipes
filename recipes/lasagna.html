Lasagna

Back to Home

Description

Lasagna is a popular Italian dish made with layers of pasta, tomato sauce, cheese, and meat or vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Boil lasagna noodles in salted water until al dente.
  3. While pasta cooks, sauté onion and garlic in butter.
  4. Combine tomato sauce, ground beef, and cooked pasta in a baking dish.
  5. Layer pasta, sauce, and cheese in the baking dish.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
