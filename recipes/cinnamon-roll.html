Cinnamon Roll

Back to Home

Description

Cinnamon roll is a sweet dessert made with a rolled-out dough, cinnamon and sugar, and often topped with cream cheese frosting.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Cinnamon
  • Butter
  • Cream cheese
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla extract
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Roll out dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread cinnamon and sugar mixture evenly on dough.
  4. Roll up dough tightly and secure with toothpicks.
  5. Place in a baking dish and brush with melted butter.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until golden brown.
